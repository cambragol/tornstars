Select Your Starting Position

Choose from the options on the left to pick where you begin in the cluster. Each option has its own benefits and advantages, though in general the choices will be balanced between difficulties based on the location, starting credits and faction relations. The Trader Start is somewhat experimental, as the option for a player career as an actual freight ship pilot is a last minute addition to Unstable Space. In Unstable Space, actions will primarily be carried out by your wingmen.
