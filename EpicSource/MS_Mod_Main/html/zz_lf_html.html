Navigation Screen

This GUI lists all known, navigable objects and locations in the Middle States.

Objects and locations are first sorted by system, and thereafter by object/location type.

Selectable filters are included to allow the player to view only the types of objects/locations they are interested in.

The main filters are:

- Show all
- Favourites
- Stations
- L-Points
- Planets

Secondary filters allow sorting of ships and fleets which are broadcasting their location.

You can add and remove entries to the favourites section by clicking the 'Add to Favourites' and 'Remove from Favourites' buttons.
