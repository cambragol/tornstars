Combat Setup 1

Fleet Composition

You are starting with a only your own tug and a single 6 pod freighter.

Task List

After loading this setup and before starting any of the tasks on this list in game, be sure to open up your menu (pressing 'o') and select 'Rest Day'. This will allow the various Operations in the system to move about. You may need to use the 'Rest Day' command several times in order to find an Operation in a suitable location to test a task. Operations can be found in the Navigation screen, located at the lower left, after clicking and selecting a location from the list at top right.

Tasks

1. Load up your transport with the pods at your start location. Proceed to Hoel Lpoint. Once there refuse the request to pay the 'toll'. Attempt to make it away safely.

2. Proceed to any Etcom, Chavez, Imperial Mining or CMCMining station in the system. Close to a distance of 20km. Then attempt to retreat safely

3. Locate a station with an Operation present. Proceed to that station and close to a distance of 20km. Then attempt to retreat safely.

4. Attempt to steal some cargo.

5. Load up your transport with the pods at your start location. Proceed to Hoel Lpoint. Attempt to run the customs toll that may be there, and pass through the Lpoint.
