Sell Claim Screen

Prospectors may sell the claims they have discovered here. After an asteroid is surveyed using a science ship, it will be claimed by you.

If you do not have the funds to exploit your discoveries, you may sell them to Megacorporations. Open this screen near a megacorporation owned station and a sell button will appear.

You can use this screen to abandon a claim at any time. Asteroids with Unusable levels of ore are not worth keeping.
