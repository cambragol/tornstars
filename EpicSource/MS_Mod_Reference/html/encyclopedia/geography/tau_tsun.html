Tau Tsun. Tau Tsun quickly became the Emperor's choice for seat of power after the brief rebellion at Emerald. This system proved to be strategically located along different lines of shipping and trade, as well as a jump point for exploration to the Badlands. The Emperor has constructed an enormous space station for his headquarters and governing center, supported by a number of smaller stations nearby. The defense of the system of considerable, as the Emperor keeps and entire fleet stationed near the Forbidden Orbital at all times. Naval training is also based here.
