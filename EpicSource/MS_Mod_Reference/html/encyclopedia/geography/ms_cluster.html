Middle States Cluster

Travel Prohibitions

The systems of Ripon and Epitaph have been declared No Travel Zones by both the Imperial Government and The Sultanate. These systems harbor hostile forces greater than typical minor criminals. Civilian trade and passenger transports are ordered to Stay Clear of these star systems.

The automated defense machines continue to survive in Epitaph. The defenses attack all ships they encounter and are very dangerous. They are mobile and have been known to pursue ships across multiple systems.

A cult of religious zealots have taken control of the Ripon system. All attempts at diplomacy have been met with hostile fire. The Brethren (as they refer to themselves) should be avoided. Captains should detour through the well patrolled systems of Watts and Kronholm.

Travel Restrictions

Travel Warnings have been issued for several systems in the Middle States region. Government services in these systems have collapsed. Criminal elements are common and rescue and repair services are unreliable. Captains are strongly cautioned against traveling these systems.

The Dead Reaches

Several star systems in the eastern Middle States have collapsed into anarchy. These systems include T'Ang, Powys, Bedalov, Alcuin, and Bumpy's Star. No governmental police operate in these systems.

Pimental and Lindevaal pirate clans operate openly in these systems. Captains are encouraged to hire security escorts.

Hazard bonuses are authorized for all cargo shipments into and out of The Dead Reaches systems.

Systems of the Dead Reaches:

T'ang

Powys

Bedalov

Alcuin

Bumpy's Star



The Barrens

Several star systems in the western Middle States have collapsed into anarchy. These systems include Hant, Akra, Lombard, Ripon, Epitaph. No governmental police operate in these systems.

Droznic and Gargoyle pirate clans operate in these systems openly. The Takanan Raiders all but control the vital trade system of Hant. Captains are strongly encouraged to join large well defended convoys.

Hazard bonuses are authorized for all cargo shipments into and out of The Barrens.

Systems of the Barrens:

Hant

Akra

Lombard

Ripon

Epitaph



Nisami/Watts Corridor

Most of the trade between The Sultanate and The Leung Empire moves up and down the Nisami/Watts corridor. These systems include the Corliss Conglomerate and the Consolidated Mining Corporation (Watts). This trade corridor is the safest passage north to south. Security patrols are frequent and waystations and shipyards provide rapid service.

Systems of the Nisami/Watts Corridor:

Corliss

Nisami

Watts



The Leung Empire

The southern Middle States is occupied by the manufacturing giant The Leung Empire. The manufacturing stations of the Empire supply most of the cluster with inexpensive goods. The Empire purchases bulk minerals and high tech goods.

The Imperial Navy maintains a strong presence. Guards and customs patrols can be found on all borders. Piracy is kept to a minimum within the Empire proper.

Systems of the Leung Empire:

Tau Tsun

Kronholm

Xu Xuan

Li Po



The Sultanate of Khalilistan

The northern Middle States is occupied by the wealthy and high tech Sultanate. The Sultanate has invested the wealth it earns from the exclusive Neutronium mines in Al Wadi into high tech manufactures. These critical high tech components are in demand throughout the cluster.

The Sultanate employs a rather corrupt but efficient mercenary force to patrol the borders and collect taxes.

Systems of the Sultanate:

Al Wadi

Aleppo

New Constantinople

Ivory Garden



The Emerald Alliance

This fledgling government has been slowly reclaiming the systems of The Dead Reaches. The government is based around the huge agricultural planet of Emerald. Emerald is the most Terran planet in the cluster and a constant source of inexpensive foodstuffs.

Systems of the Emerald Alliance:

An-Chi (Emerald)

Huang

Providence

Penda



The Caliphate of Yezhid

The most 'Wester' reaches of the cluster are controlled by the Caliphate of Yezhid. This small power is a breakaway state of the Sultanate of Khalilistan. The system of Nureddin is still disputed between the two powers, and is the site of frequent skirmishes. Currently the Caliphate is struggling to rebuild it's systems, and to expand it's economic strength and diversity. The seat of government is situated in Nureddin, in orbit around the moon of Jiddah.

Systems of the Caliphate:

Nureddin

Saladin
