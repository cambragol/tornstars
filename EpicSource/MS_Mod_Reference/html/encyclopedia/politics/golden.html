Golden Lotus

Base System:  Li Po

Political Foes:  The Golden Lotus has strained relations with many factions operating within the Emerald Alliance, and is attacked on sight by the Emerald Navy. 

Description:  The Golden Lotus is a shadowy organization, which seems to operate with a fair amount of impunity within the borders of the Leung Empire.  This is despite that fact that they are known to be criminal in organization, and quite ruthless.
