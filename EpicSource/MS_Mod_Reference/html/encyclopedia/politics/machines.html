Epitaph Defence Machines

Base System:  Epitaph

Political Foes:  The automated defence machines of Epitaph attack all factions, except the Gargoyles..

Description:  The defense machines are automated mining and manufacturing systems that were used to ambush the Leung Fleet in the War of Dissolution.  The hasty conversion to weapons let them get out of control. No one can approach them to reprogram them. And they keep rebuilding themselves.  The gargoyles seem to have discovered how to coexists. Which gives them a perfect base to raid from.
