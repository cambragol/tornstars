CMC Security Services

Capitol System:  Watts

Political Foes:  CMC has very strained relations with the Leung Empire, but there are no open hostilities.  The Corliss Conglomerate should also be considered a primary rival of the CMC.  Currently the CMC is engaged in low level hostilities with the Droznic crime family and the Takanan Raiders.

Description:  The CMC is the largest remaining single mega-corporation in the cluster, and can be counted on to act in its best corporate interests regardless of history, alliances, or emotions. It is one of the oldest corporations in this cluster, having helped open it for settlement back when they were operating in partnership with Emperor Leung. Those days are long past, and at present there is no love lost between the CMC and the Leung Empire.  The security service arm of CMC is both a police force and navy for the Watts system.
