Caliphate of Yezhid

Capitol System:  Nureddin

Provincial Systems:  Saladin

Political Foes:  The Caliphate of Yezhid ever suffers under the attacks of the Takanan Raiders, and engages them upon sight.  Tensions with the Sultanate are very high as well, and further provocations might trigger a war.  The Caliphate also has low regard for the CMC, whom have failed them in their efforts against the Takanan Raiders.

Description:  Nureddin was spared the destruction of the War of Dissolution, but was gutted shortly afterward by the treaty reparation costs and exodus of its population. It was abandoned by the Sultanate in its time of crisis, causing discontent in the population remaining behind.  Though it is off the main trade route, Nureddin is often visited by trade convoys for re-fueling and re-supply.  The Caliphate of Yezhid also includes the Saladdin system, which was essentially wiped out during the War. It has taken over 70 years to rebuild its stations and industry base to a point where it could be considered viable again.  The Caliphate of Yezhid is primarily focused on the neighbouring Sultanate, but they are also aware of the importance of their relationship with the Corliss Conglomerate, despite the distance and Barren system of Hant separating them.
