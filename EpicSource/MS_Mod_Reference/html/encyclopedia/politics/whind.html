Whind Weapon Systems

Corporate Headquarters:  Based in the Badlands

Operations Systems:  Maintains operations throughout the Middle States.

Political Foes:  Excluding pirates and raiders, Whind Weapon Systems has fair relations with most factions.

Description:  This is a Corporation based in the Badlands, but with franchise branches located throughout the Middle States, with dealerships everywhere offering top-of-the-line ship weapon systems. Freighters carrying inventory can occasionally be spotted and relieved of their cargo if their escort can be eliminated.
