Universal Consumer Products

Corporate Headquarters:  Local headquarters in Emerald and Corliss

Operations Systems:  Shipping arm found operating throughout the Middle States.

Political Foes:  Excluding pirates and raiders, Universal Consumer Products has fair relations with most factions.

Description:  This Corporation has its Head Office in the Badlands, but operates local field offices at Corliss and Emerald. They supply the people of the Middle States with many of the consumer and manufactured products they need to thrive in an interstellar society. While they have their own shipping network, they often make use of third party or independent carriers to transport their products into more dangerous regions of space
