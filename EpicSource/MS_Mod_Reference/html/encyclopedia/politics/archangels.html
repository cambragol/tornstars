Archangels Mercenary

Base System:  Unknown; somewhere in the Sultanate of Khalilistan.

Political Foes:  The Archangels are engaged in open hostilities with the Gargoyles, but are not well loved by many factions that ply the lanes between Kronholm and New Constantinople.

Description:  This organization is well trained and well equipped, operating from a hidden asteroid base somewhere within the Sultanate of Khalilistan. These wealthy and proud mercenaries operate under the employ of the Sultan, and are largely responsible for the defense (and offensive dirty work) of the Sultanate. They have enjoyed this privileged position for decades, much to the dismay of many other mercenary clans interested in the contract, and the many enemies they've made over the years as the Sultan's henchmen.
