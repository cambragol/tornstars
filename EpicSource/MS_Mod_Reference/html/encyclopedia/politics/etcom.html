EtCom Corporation

Corporate Headquarters:  Alcuin

Operations Systems:  Alcuin, Bedalov, Bumpy's Star, Emerald

Political Foes:  Tensions are high with the Azran Corporation.

Description:  EtCom Corporation is based in Alcuin, and has been sharing the resources of that system with the Azran Corporation for some time.  EtCom, however, is expanding well beyond the Dead Reaches, selling communications services cluster-wide and branching out to provide ship communications technology and improved computer systems.  The long strained relations between EtCom and Azran have also taken a turn for a worse of late, and there is a potential for outright warfare, to determine who shall finally inherit Alcuin.
