Grinnel Freightlines

Corporate Headquarters:  New Constantinople

Operations Systems:  Throughout the Middle States.

Political Foes:  Excluding pirates and raiders, Grinnel Freightlines has fair relations with most factions.

Description:  Grinnel is an older name in the Middle States cluster shipping lanes. They'll haul anything anywhere, guarantee safety with their fine hired escorts, and do so at reasonable prices. Their favoured clientele include the systems of the Sultanate, the Caliphate of Yezhid, and the Corliss Conglomerate.
