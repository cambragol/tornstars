Markus Corporation

Corporate Headquarters:  Nisami

Operations Systems:  Nisami, Corliss

Political Foes:  Excluding pirates and raiders, Markus Corporation has fair relations with most factions.

Description:  The Markus Corporation, based in Nisami, is involved in a wide range of activities and products, but it has the reputation as a company that does not fear taking risks. Markus Corporation's genetics division is engaged in longevity research.  The Markus Corporation has also shown an inexplicable interest in the exploration of the jump routes back to Sirius, funding a number of seemingly pointless probe missions from the T'Ang system.
