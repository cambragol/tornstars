Uncleansed

Base System:  No base system; found throughout the Leung Empire, Nisami-Watts corridor and even parts of the Emerald Alliance and Dead Reaches.

Description:  This is a descriptor that has become a label as well as an IFF tag for a class of citizenry within the Leung Empire.  Regarded with contempt by all, the 'Uncleansed' have been forced to the outskirts of society.  They survive doing the jobs and tasks that others deem low, or some how unclean.  It is also commonly known that many of the 'Uncleansed' turn to crime in order to survive.  Though not organized in the fashion of a true faction, the 'Uncleansed' will come to each others aid when threatened.
