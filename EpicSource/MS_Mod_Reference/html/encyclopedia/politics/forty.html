Forty Thieves

Base System:  Ivory Gardens;  They are found throughout the Sultanate and as far as the Caliphate.

Political Foes:  The Forty Thieves are very hostile towards the ships of the Caliphate. 

Description:  Once the scourge of the Sultanate, the Forty Thieves have perhaps bought them selves some breathing room in their home systems by focusing their predations upon the Caliphate.  This has done nothing to endear them to the people of those systems.
