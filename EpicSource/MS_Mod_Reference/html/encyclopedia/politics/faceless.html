Faceless

Base System:  No base system; found throughout the Sultanate and Caliphate, and their border systems.

Description:  This is a descriptor that has become a label as well as an IFF tag for a class of citizenry within the Sultanate and Caliphate.  Though outwardly assuming a legitimate roll as traders, haulers, or miners, it is commonly assumed that they have a criminal element as well.  The 'Faceless' are the lowest 'caste' within both the Caliphate and Sultanate.  Whether this and the accompanying derision, contempt and bigotry they are on the receiving end of is the cause of their criminal reputation, or they actually earn this label through nefarious means, is generally unknown.  The truth matters little for they will still be regarded as the lowest of the low.  The label 'Faceless' has a further meaning, referring to those whom have no honor or courage.  Despite this, they do show honor to one another, and will not hesitate to band together when attacked.
