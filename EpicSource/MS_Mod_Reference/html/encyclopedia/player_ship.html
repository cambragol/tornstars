Your Ship

The ship you are flying has some special features. You can change the load out of weapons it carries. You can upgrade the ship by purchasing a new ship at select stations.

Loadout

You can change the loadout of your ship after you enter a station. Dock to the station. Then use the Ship Computer menu to Enter Station. You will be taken into the station. Use the hanger inside to change the loadout of your ship.

New Ship

You can purchase new Tugs at Shipyards. Patcoms and Corvettes can be purchased at Navy Defense stations. You must have the cash and standing needed to purchase a new ship.

Dock to the station and then use the Purchase Ship menu item on the Ship Computer menu. You will be given access to your new ship from within the station. Select your ship from the Hangar then simply launch it.

Life Pod

If your ship is destroyed you will be ejected in a small life pod. This tiny vessel is unarmed and very fragile. It has LDS drive but no Capsule jump ability.

You will be killed if your life pod is destroyed. If you do not have the funds to replace your ship, your career as a ship captain is over. If you are caught in a system with no friendly stations where you can purchase a ship, your career is over.

Equipment Upgrades

You can purchase new equipment and weapons for your ship from Traders and Black Market Traders. These traders fly ships near stations and start a cyan color on your contact list. Different traders, of different factions will be able to offer different types of equipment or weapons. Only by seeking them out and gaining their factions trust will you be able to equip your ship with the best of the best.

Use the ship to ship communication system to start a dialog. If they like you, they will let you purchase equipment.

Trade ships can be found near shipyards, mercenary bases, navy bases or police stations.

Black market traders can be found near pirate stations.

Your Equipment Inventory

Your ship equipment is not stored in cargo pods and does not need to be transported. It is kept in storage inside your ship equipment lockers. This equipment can be remounted at any station where you have docked your ship.

Cargo pods cannot be stored in these equipment lockers.
