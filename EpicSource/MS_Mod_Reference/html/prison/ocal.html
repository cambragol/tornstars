Tseng. Tseng quickly became the Emperor's choice for seat of power after the brief rebellion at Emerald. The Tau Tsun system, in which Tseng is located, proved to be strategically located along different lines of shipping and trade, as well as a jump point for exploration to the Badlands. The Emperor has constructed an enormous space station for his headquarters and governing center, supported by a number of smaller stations nearby. The defense of the system of considerable, as the Emperor keeps and entire fleet stationed near the Forbidden Orbital at all times. Naval training is also based here.

Supplies: Trained naval officers, security forces, manufactured goods, consumer items. 

Demands: Manufactured goods, luxury foods, luxury items, art, technical advisors, contraband.
