Tulunid (New Constantinople) This system is the capital, and the seat of power of the Sultanate. The first Sultan (Malik Shah) chose this system for its mineral wealth, its proximity to Al Wadi. It also seemed a good choice for the Mars-like world of New Constantinople, which was relatively friendly to colonization and amenable to terraforming once the biobombers could be built. Plans to terraform it were drawn up, but have yet to get started. The capital city and the Sultan's palace are under protective domes, which were fortified, and are now heavily defended due to the military buildup by the second Sultan Abu Tammam. From here, the new Sultan Salazar and his sister manage the remnants of the Sultanate by using the vast riches their family has accumulated, and a notorious spy network. Numerous stations and processing facilities can be found in this system, as well as the Sultan’s main shipyard. This is one of the few systems in the Middle States Cluster that has a vast library and a vibrant, reputable university: The University of Tulun.

Supplies: Arts, refined ores, refined exotic metals, refined gases, refined neutronium, metal rods, some manufactured goods, novel navigational and materials science data.

Demands: Luxury items, water,agricultural products, station components, hull plates, ship systems, propulsion systems, weapon systems, terraforming equipment, electronics, nanotech repair systems, people (immigrant skilled and unskilled workforces, mercenaries, researchers, manufacturing specialists, clerical, service industries), books, archives, illicit drugs, longevity pharmaceuticals.
