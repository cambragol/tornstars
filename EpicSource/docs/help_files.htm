This screen displays the accounting information for your fleet.

 

Select a ship in the list on the left for details.

 

The Daily Upkeep is the daily cost of operating a ship (ship repair, crew, and spare parts).

 

The Fuel Per Jump is the amount of fuel needed to capsule jump the ship from one system to another. The cost is in credits. Capsule jumps within the same system are free.

 

Fuel Used is the amount of fuel the ship has used jumping, but has not yet charged your account. This amount will be charged at end of the day.

 

Funds displays the amount of money you have in your accounts.

 

Each capsule jump from system to system takes one day to complete. You will be billed for both the jump fuel and the daily upkeep cost of the ship. If you cannot pay for your wingmen’s fuel or upkeep they will desert.

 

You can fire a wingman at any time by using the communication system (target the wingman and press “c”).

 

 

Reputation Gui

 

This screen displays your relation level to each faction in the Middle States.

 

All the factions that you currently know about are listed. Click on a faction name for a description of the faction.

 

The Upgrade Cost is the price you need to pay in fees (or bribes) to improve your standing with the faction. Higher relation levels cost more credits.

 

The Upgrade button will charge the price listed and upgrade your faction standing.

 

Your relation level determines how the faction treats you.

 

Hostile standing means the faction will attack you on site. Once a faction has turned hostile it cannot be upgraded. Factions do not give second chances to those they consider pirates.

 

Dislike standing means the faction will not attack you on site, but they are unlikely to sell you any cargo. They may not be willing to work for you either.

 

Neutral standing means the faction will sell you basic low cost goods. Most small ships will be willing to work for you.

 

Friendly standing means the faction will sell you higher priced goods. Most ships of the faction will be willing to work for you.

 

Allied standing means that the faction will sell you any goods it produces. All of the ships of the faction will be willing to work for you.

 

Hidden Factions

Criminal factions are keep a low profile and information is sketchy. They will not appear in the Reputation Screen until you have docked to one of their stations and collected data.

 

Ship Dealer

 

You can replace or upgrade your ship at stations that support a ship dealer.

 

These stations are the Shipyard, Waystation, Mercenary Base, and the System Defense Dock.

 

The ships available for sale are listed on the upper left. Click on a ship for a description. The price of the ship and your current funds are listed below the ships.

 

Press the Buy Ship button to purchase the new ship. Your old ship will be sold. You will be taken inside the station where you can change the loadout of your new ship. You may only own one ship at a time.

 

You can sell your current ship if you choose. You will be taken inside the station and provided with a life pod.

 

Launch from the station to begin using your new ship.

 

Erase Your Identity

 

Erasing your identity will allow you a fresh start in the Middle States. Genetic restructuring and organ transplants allow you to take on the identity of a kidnapped citizen of The Dead Reaches.

 

Your faction standings will be reset. You will lose all your upgraded factions, but you will also no longer be at Open War with any Governments or Corporations.

 

Your current ship will be sold (to prevent it being traced to your new identity) and you will be taken inside the station and given a life pod.
